## FAQ — English

**How much does a wedding film cost?**
Wedding packages start from €1.200 (film only) or €1.800 (film + photos). Final pricing depends on location, duration and package. Contact for a personalised quote.

**Do you travel for projects?**
Yes — Mato works internationally. Projects within Oberösterreich have no travel fee. All other locations are quoted individually.

**How long until I receive the final video?**
Typically 6–10 weeks after the event for weddings. Commercial projects: 2–4 weeks.

**Can I see examples of your work?**
Yes — visit the portfolio section on matografie.at or mato.ba.

**Do you shoot photos as well as video?**
Yes — the wedding film + photo package includes both cinematic video and a full edited photo gallery.

**Is a deposit required?**
Yes — a deposit is required to confirm the booking. Details are included in the offer.

**Can I request specific songs for the film?**
Yes — Mato works with licensed music and can discuss your preferences during the brief.

**What format will I receive the files in?**
Video delivered as MP4 (H.264/H.265), 4K resolution. Photos as high-resolution JPEGs.
